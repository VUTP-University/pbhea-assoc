Founding Institutions

The Association “Positioning Bulgarian Higher Education Abroad” (PBHEA) was founded by four leading academic institutions in Bulgaria, each with a strong international profile and a commitment to excellence in education and research.

🌐 1. University of Telecommunications and Post (UTP), Sofia
  • A modern university specializing in digital leadership, cybersecurity, logistics, and smart technologies.
  • Actively involved in innovation, entrepreneurship, and cooperation with business and government.
  • Known for international partnerships and EU project participation.
🏛️ 2. University of Mining and Geology “St. Ivan Rilski”, Sofia
  • Expertise in mining, geology, energy, and environmental protection.
  • Pioneer in geoscience education and applied research.
  • Focus on sustainable development and digital transformation.
🔬 3. Medical University of Pleven
  • One of the most advanced medical universities in Southeast Europe.
  • English-taught programs in Medicine and Healthcare.
  • Recognized for robotic surgery training and digital health research.
🎓 4. Agricultural University – Plovdiv
  • The leading agricultural higher education institution in Bulgaria.
  • Focus on biotechnology, agribusiness, climate-smart agriculture.
  • Promotes mobility, green transition, and food innovation.
